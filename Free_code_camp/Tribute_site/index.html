Where did the name come from?
When this fruit was discovered in America, the Europeans named them Pineapples because they looked similar to Pine Cones
What is a Pineapple?
A Pineapple is not an apple or pine, it's actually a berry
How long do they take to grow?
An individual Pineapple can take over two years to grow, although they are usually picked slightly eariler
How long do Pineapple Plants take to grow?
In the wild, Pinapple plants can live and fruit for up to 50 years
How can you grow a Pineapple?
You can grow Pineapple Plants by slicing off the top of a pineapple and planting it in the soil
How big was the largest Pineapple?
The world's largest Pineapple ever recored was in 2011. It weighed 8.28kg
Where are they grown?
75% of all Pineapples sold in Europe are grown entirely in Costa Rica
What is another name for Pineapples?
The Hawaiian word for Pineapple in 'halakahiki'
